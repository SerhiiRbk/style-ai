## Executive summary (paste into any form)

Valetti (valetti.fit) is an AI-native personal styling platform for EU and USA. Users upload photos; our Style Recommendation Engine builds a Style Profile, generates looks, matches 5,000+ catalog SKUs, and supports virtual try-on.

We seek credits to fund inference at early scale (~500–2,000 reports/month). Variable COGS per paid report: €0.34–1.08. Credits extend runway while we grow €10–35 paid conversion.

**Stack:** Vercel · Supabase · Claude (vision/reasoning) · Gemini (images) · OpenAI embeddings · fal.ai (try-on)
